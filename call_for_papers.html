Call for Papers: ICDCC 2024

The International Conference on Data Computation and Communication (ICDCC 2024) is pleased to invite researchers, scholars, and practitioners to submit their original and unpublished work to the upcoming conference. This prestigious event will bring together leading experts from around the world to discuss the latest trends, technologies, and innovations in the fields of data computation and communication. We encourage contributions in the following streams, but submissions are not limited to these topics:

1. Data Computation

Novel algorithms for data processing and analysis

Computational methods for large-scale data

Data mining and machine learning techniques

Optimization methods in data computation

Security and privacy in data computation

2. Data Communication

• Advances in network protocols and architectures

• Wireless and mobile communication technologies

• Data transmission techniques

• Internet of Things (IoT) communication strategies

• Cybersecurity in data communication

3. Applications of Data Computation and Communication

• Real-world applications in healthcare, finance, and industry

• Smart cities and intelligent transportation systems

• Data-driven decision-making processes

• Computational and communication models for social networks

• Case studies of data computation and communication in practice

4. Technological Advances and Innovations

• Emerging technologies in data computation and communication

• Blockchain and distributed ledger technologies

• Quantum computing and its applications in data computation

• Artificial intelligence and machine learning advancements

• Innovations in hardware and software for data systems

5. Interdisciplinary Perspectives

• Cross-disciplinary approaches combining data computation and communication

• Integration of computational techniques in the humanities and social sciences

• Ethical and societal implications of data technologies

• Policy and regulatory challenges in data-driven technologies

• Collaboration between academia and industry in data research

Submission Guidelines:

All submissions must be original and not under review by any other conference or publication. Papers should be formatted according to the conference template and submitted electronically through the ICDCC 2024 submission portal. Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings.

Submission Guidelines:

Paper Submission Deadline: October 25, 2024

Notification of Acceptance: November 05, 2024

Camera-Ready Submission: November 11, 2024

Conference Dates: November 29-30, 2024

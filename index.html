Chief Guest

Prof. Gobardhan Das

Ph.D, D.Sc, FAScT, FNASc, FASc
Director, IISER Bhopal

Prof. Gobardhan Das is the Director of Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Bhopal (IISER Bhopal). Prior to assuming this position, Prof. Das was working as a Professor and Chair of Special Centre for Molecular Medicine at Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi.
Prof. Das completed his doctorate (Ph.D.) in Immunology - Host pathogen relationship, from the Institute of Microbial Technology, Chandigarh in 1997. He is associated with reputed professional organizations such as Molecular Immunology Forum, and Guha Research Council. Prof. Das also holds editorial positions as associate editor in various prestigious journals.
Prof Das inspirational leadership has the potential to create a ripple effect of positive change in the institute. By fostering a culture of innovation, collaboration, personal growth, and adaptability, the institute can experience significant growth and development under his guidance.

Guest of Honor

Amitabh Tiwari

BE (Electrical Engineering), MANIT Bhopal; MBA, IIM Bangalore
Assurance Head, TCS

Amitabh Tiwari is the Assurance Head at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS). He has extensive experience in the IT and consulting sectors, having held various roles at renowned organizations before joining TCS. His prior positions include being a Program Director at American Express and a Project Manager at financial institutions like Merrill Lynch, Barclays Investment Bank, and JPMorgan Chase, showcasing his expertise in managing large-scale programs and projects across industries​.
At TCS, his role involves spearheading quality assurance initiatives and driving innovation in enterprise solutions. TCS has been recognized as a leader in quality assurance services under his leadership, particularly in areas like next-generation technologies and domain-specific solutions for industries such as BFSI (Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance) and healthcare​.

About ICDCC-2024

Welcome to ICDCC-2024

The "International Conference on Data Computation and Communication" (ICDCC) is a significant event in the field of data science, computation, and communication technology. Conferences like these typically bring together researchers, academics, industry experts, and practitioners from around the world to discuss and exchange ideas on the latest advancements, trends, and challenges in the field. The technical program includes keynote speeches, plenary talks, regular technical sessions, and special sessions.

About VIT,Bhopal

Welcome to VIT, Bhopal

VIT Bhopal University is part of the Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) group of institutions, offering a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. Established in 2017, it is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and located in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, India. The university offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across fields such as engineering, management, computer applications, science, and humanities. These programs are designed to meet industry demands and prepare students for global careers. The curriculum is crafted to include theoretical foundations as well as practical applications, ensuring holistic development and employability. VIT Bhopal University prides itself on its state-of-the-art infrastructure, equipped with modern facilities including classrooms, laboratories, libraries, sports complexes, and residential accommodation. The campus provides a conducive environment for learning, research, and extracurricular activities. The university places a strong emphasis on research and innovation, encouraging faculty and students to engage in cutting-edge research projects...
